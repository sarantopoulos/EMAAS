Our History

Founded in Sparta

Established by 22 internationally renowned scientists and artists in the historic city of Sparta, Greece, with headquarters there and a branch in Lausanne, Switzerland.[attached_file:1]

Global Expansion

Emboldened by years of success, EMAAS plans further branch offices worldwide, focusing on global innovation, academic excellence, and sustainable development.[attached_file:1]
