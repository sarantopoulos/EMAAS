# EMAAS
EMAAS (Euro Mediterranean Academy of Arts and Sciences) is a global academy dedicated to ensuring high-quality research by selected scholars who advance sciences for worldwide and personal excellence. Its vision is to be a leading international academy that sets a high standard for global knowledge.

Founded by 22 renowned scientists and artists in Sparta, Greece, with a branch in Lausanne, Switzerland, EMAAS plans to expand further. The academy focuses on global innovation through inclusive research, building a strong network of excellence, and promoting strategic sustainable development.
